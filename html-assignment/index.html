About Me

My name is Nathan Templeton and I am a student at Algonquin College. I am a person who enjoys learning new things, with myself having learnt many hobbies in my freetime. I have become proficient with many instruments including the piano, guitar, saxophone and harmonica. I enjoy playing piano the most in my free time and like to improvise and try to learn new songs written or by ear. I am also a great artist, as drawing was a skill that I had picked up during the pandemic. I enjoy sketching characters and landscapes in fantasy, but also relish in drawing still lifes and things of that nature. Alongside these pastimes I also enjoy many other of the finer things in life like reading, exercising, video games, 3D modeling, and of course eating good food.

Many of these new skills were not yet learnt by me before the Covid-19 pandemic. I can still remember the day of March 17th, 2020, when my high school shut down. Naturally being a young teenager with no responsibilities and school for months, I resorted to playing video games all day. While fun at first, playing Animal Crossing and FF for far too long can start messing with your head and I decided to make a change and begin improving myself. I decided to adopt this mindset,

It is never too late to be who you might have been -George Elliot.
I started to lose weight, and learn to draw from the ground up. Since starting my self-improvement journey I have felt much more fulfilled and proud in my life.
